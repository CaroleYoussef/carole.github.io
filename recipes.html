Koshari - Traditional Egyptian Street Food

Egyptian
Prep: 20 min | Cook: 25 min | Total: 45 min
Difficulty: Medium
Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 cup brown lentils
  • 1 cup elbow macaroni
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained
  • 2 large onions, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp coriander
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Cook rice according to package instructions. Set aside.
  2. Cook lentils in salted water until tender. Drain and set aside.
  3. Cook macaroni according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a large pan, heat olive oil and fry onions until golden brown and crispy. Remove half for garnish.
  5. In the same pan, add garlic and spices, cook for 1 minute.
  6. Add tomato paste and cook for 2 minutes, then add chickpeas and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Layer rice, lentils, and macaroni in a serving dish.
  8. Top with the tomato-chickpea mixture and garnish with crispy onions.
  9. Serve hot with additional spices and hot sauce if desired.

Tips & Notes

  • You can prepare the components ahead of time and assemble just before serving.
  • For extra flavor, add a squeeze of lemon juice before serving.
  • Traditional koshari is served with a spicy tomato sauce on the side.
